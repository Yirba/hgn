Higanbana no Saku Yoru Ni is the latest sound novel series by 07th Expansion, creators of the When They Cry series - Umineko no Naku Koro Ni and Higurashi no Naku Koro Ni. Both series have had translators tackle them over the years, most notably the group Witch-Hunt, which has currently almost finished translating all 8 episodes of Umineko. Also notable is the recent translation by Manga Gamer of Higurashi. If you haven't read an 07th Expansion novel before, be sure to try the free demo of Umineko that has been translated by Witch-Hunt. Or try out the free demo of Higurashi provided by Manga Gamer.

Our translation project exists to support and spread the work of 07th Expansion to the English speaking community, particularly Higanbana no Saku Yoru Ni. Do not use our patches on illegal copies of Higanbana no Saku Yoru Ni - The First Night, but rather buy the game and promote the work of this doujin group in your own country.
